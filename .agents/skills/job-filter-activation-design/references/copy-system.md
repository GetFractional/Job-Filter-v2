# Copy System

## Core Jobs

Every screen should answer:

1. What is happening?
2. Why does it matter?
3. What happens next?

## Tone

- Warm
- Grounded
- Clear
- Premium without theatrics

## Prefer

- Build your profile once. Use it everywhere.
- Confirm your experience.
- Keep every claim grounded in facts you approve.
- No resume? Start manually.
- Use recommended version.
- Excluded from use.
- Why we inferred this.

## Avoid

- Highlight
- Outcome when “Result” is clearer
- Rejected when “Excluded from use” is safer
- Parser, raw source, normalized source in the main path
- Generic “faster, smarter, better” claims without mechanism

## Belief Shift

- From: “This is setup work.”
- To: “This creates the source of truth that saves me time and improves my applications.”
